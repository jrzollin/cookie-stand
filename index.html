About the Cookies!

Pat's cookies have become quite the popular attraction in the Seattle area! Each cookie is carefully shaped like that distinct looking fish and baked with love to give you that warm feeling when you eat one! Wondering what all the hubbub is about? Try one! Trying to guess that secret ingredient? Well, you're right! It's salmon! Delicous, delicious salmon.
